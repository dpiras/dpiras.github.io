I am now a Resarch Fellow at UCL, working with Prof. Hiranya Peiris and Prof. Andrew Pontzen Prof. Andrew Pontzen on explainable AI techniques applied to cosmology.

I did my PhD in the Centre for Doctoral Training in Data Intensive Science at University College London, funded by STFC (STFC UCL CDT in DIS, if you like acronyms). I worked under the supervision of Prof Benjamin Joachimi and Prof. John Shawe-Taylor, at the intersection between cosmology and machine learning.

My research involves developing and applying machine learning algorithms to large simulations of our Universe. In a typical day, I employ advanced statistical techniques to analyse big data, I code in python and I try to convince people that pineapple pizza should not be a thing - yes, I'm Italian.
