I am a PhD student in the Centre for Doctoral Training in Data Intensive Science at University College London, funded by STFC (STFC UCL CDT in DIS, if you like acronyms). I work under the supervision of Dr Benjamin Joachimi and Prof. John Shawe-Taylor, at the intersection between Cosmology and Machine Learning.

My research involves developing and applying machine learning tecnhiques to the biggest simulations of our Universe. In a typical day, I employ advanced statistical techniques to analyse big data, I code in python and I try to convince people that pineapple pizza should not be a thing - yes, I'm Italian.
